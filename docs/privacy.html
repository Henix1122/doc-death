Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Death ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username, email address, and password
  • Profile information (bio, profile picture, display name)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Date of birth

Content You Create

We collect and store:

  • Posts, stories, and comments
  • Photos, videos, and other media you upload
  • Messages and conversations
  • Metadata (timestamps, location data if enabled)

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our app:

  • Device information (model, OS, app version)
  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Feature usage analytics

Third-Party Information

If you use social login (Google, Apple, Facebook), we receive your profile information from those services with your consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Create accounts, deliver messages, store content
  • Improve Experience: Personalize feeds, recommend content, fix bugs
  • Safety & Security: Detect fraud, enforce terms, prevent abuse
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns (anonymized data)
  • Communication: Send service updates, respond to support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:

  • With Service Providers: Cloud storage, analytics, payment processors
  • For Legal Reasons: Subpoenas, court orders, or to protect rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy
  • Publicly (if you choose): Your profile and content are visible to other users

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time through Settings. Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes for up to 90 days.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) to protect data in transit. Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms. However, no online service is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication if available.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and marketing communications in Settings
  • Correction: Update your profile information anytime

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services (analytics, cloud hosting, payment processing). These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

8. Children's Privacy

Death is not intended for users under 13. If we discover a child's information, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians can contact us to request deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of Death means you accept the updated policy.
